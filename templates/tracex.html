TraceX Challenge

Team Composition

1 to 4 players per team

Objective

Build a Line Following Robot that navigates a track with obstacles

Event Details

  • Track includes obstacles and checkpoints
  • Each robot's completion time will be recorded
  • Multiple rounds based on time trials

Rules & Guidelines

  • Robots must be ready when the team is called
  • Each team allowed three touches/resets during the run
  • Resets incur a time penalty
  • Timer continues during resets
  • Organizers will inspect and approve each robot

Disqualifications

  • False start: Counter restarted, one second chance
  • Second false start leads to disqualification

Winning Criteria

  • Fastest completion time of the track
  • Number of checkpoints cleared (if times are close)
