Escape Room Challenge

Team Composition

4 players per team ‚Äî 2 in Room 1, 2 in Room 2 (after Room 1 is cleared)

Rules

  • No electronic devices (phones, calculators, etc.)
  • Room 1 players can't communicate with Room 2 players until first puzzle is solved
  • No tampering with other teams' puzzles or solutions
  • 15 minute time limit to escape

Disqualifications

  • Using mobile devices or external help
  • Tampering with props or breaking the setup
  • Interfering with other teams
  • Exceeding the time limit

Winning Criteria

  • Fastest team to solve both rooms wins
  • If time runs out, most progress determines the winner
  • In case of tie, extra challenge decides the winner
