Tech Arena Challenge

Round 1: Tech Riddles (Elimination Round)

  • Solve 10 tech-based riddles in 10 minutes
  • Only top-performing teams advance

Round 2: Code Combat

  • Face two coding challenges and one debugging problem
  • Speed and accuracy determine finalists

Round 3: Tech Gladiator Showdown

  • Buzzer-based Q&A round followed by tech debate
  • Highest-scoring team earns Tech Arena Champions title

Rules & Regulations

  • Teams must report 15 minutes before event starts
  • No mobile phones or reference materials allowed
  • Participants must maintain decorum and respect quizmasters and teams
  • Judges' decisions are final and binding
  • Rule violations may lead to disqualification
