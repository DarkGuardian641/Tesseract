Clash of Minds Debate

Team Composition

2 members per team (One For, One Against)

Topic & Time

  • Topic given 1 hour before the event
  • Each speaker gets 4 minutes to present their argument
  • 1-minute rebuttal after both speakers have spoken
  • Language: English

Judging Criteria

  • Content - Strength and depth of arguments
  • Clarity - Articulation and expression
  • Logic - Coherence and reasoning
  • Rebuttal - Countering the opponent's arguments
  • Presentation - Confidence and delivery

Disqualifications

  • Reading directly from notes
  • Misconduct or inappropriate behavior
  • Violation of competition rules
