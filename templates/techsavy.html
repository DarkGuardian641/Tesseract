Tech Savvy Quiz

Description

Participants will compete with their IoT-based line-tracing car & the one to reach the end.

Team Composition

4 participants per team

Rounds

Round 1 (General): No buzzer, 3 questions per team, 10 marks each

Round 2 (Rapid Fire): 1 min per team, max 10 questions, 5 marks per correct answer

Round 3 (Logical Thinking): Buzzer round, 10 MCQs, 15 marks for correct, -10 for wrong/no answers

Rules & Regulations

  • Strict time adherence for each round
  • Teams must report 15 minutes before the quiz starts
  • No mobile phones or reference materials allowed
  • Participants must maintain decorum and respect
  • Judges' decision is final and binding
  • Rule violations may lead to immediate disqualification
  • Discussions should be quick and quiet

Additional Information

Tie-Breaker: Special round in case of tie
