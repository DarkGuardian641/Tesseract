RoboRace Challenge

Eligibility & Team Size

  • Open to Engineering, Science, and Technical college students
  • Max 4 members per team

Robot Specifications

  • Manual control only (no automation)
  • Max size: 20 cm x 20 cm x 20 cm
  • Max weight: 2 kg
  • Powered by any safe energy source

Track & Race Rules

  • 10m track with turns, obstacles, ramps, and checkpoints
  • Penalties: 10 sec per obstacle hit, 30 sec for going off-track
  • Disqualification for inactivity (30 sec) or rule violations

Event Format

  • Qualification Round: Top 10 teams proceed to finals
  • Final Round: Head-to-head races determine the winner

Awards

  • Winner & Runner-up: Trophy, certificate, cash prize
